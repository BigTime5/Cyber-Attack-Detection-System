Project Motivation & Background

The Growing Threat

Cyber threats have evolved into one of the most significant challenges facing organizations worldwide. From sophisticated malware and phishing attacks to devastating Denial-of-Service (DDoS) incidents, these threats compromise sensitive data and disrupt critical operations on an unprecedented scale.

Limitations of Traditional Methods

Conventional security measures struggle to keep pace with rapidly evolving attack vectors. Signature-based detection systems fail against novel threats, while manual analysis cannot process the massive volumes of network traffic data generated daily.

Deep Learning Solution

Our deep learning approach analyzes network traffic patterns to identify anomalies that may indicate cyber attacks. By learning from vast datasets, neural networks can detect subtle patterns invisible to traditional methods, enabling proactive threat mitigation.

Objective

Develop and train a deep learning model capable of classifying network events as Benign (0) or Malicious (1), achieving high detection rates while minimizing false alarms.

Key Challenge: Extreme Class Imbalance
